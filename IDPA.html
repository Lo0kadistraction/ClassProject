What is IDPA

When describing a shooting sport it is always better to see it. These videos below do a pretty good job showing you the sport of IDPA. IDPA is a tactical what if game. The idea is to test your skills in a tactical environment. Because of this the game of IDPA concentrates on tactical procedures and techniques. IDPA is slower and more focused on not breaking rules because they hurt your score. the biggest difference between IDPA and USPSA is speed and how you engage a course of fire. Make sure to check out the Equipment page and the Getting Started page if you would like to try IDPA.
