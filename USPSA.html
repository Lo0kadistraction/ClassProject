What is USPSA

When describing a shooting sport, it is always better to see it to get a better understanding of it. These videos below do a pretty good at job showing you the sport of USPSA. The key takeaways are USPSA is a speed and accuracy sport with several different divisions. The name of the game is how to navigate the course the fastest way possible, while being very accurate with your shots. Make sure to check out the Equipment page and the Getting Started page if you would like to try USPSA.
